About Me

I am currently a third-year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Ying Wei and Prof. Chen Liu. I obtained my B.S. degrees in Computer Science and Technology as well as in Financial Engineering from Sichuan University.

My research interests include Meta-Learning, Out-of-Distribution, and Robustness for multimodal large language models.
